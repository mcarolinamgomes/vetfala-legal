Account Deletion Request

Managed by Maria Gomes

At VetFala (VetScribe), we value your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to stop using our service and delete your account, you can do so directly within the application.

Warning: This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to all patient histories and clinical reports generated within the app.

Option 1: Delete via App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data is through the mobile application settings:

  1. Open the VetFala app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll down and tap the red Delete Account button.
  4. Confirm your choice when prompted.

Your data will be immediately queued for deletion.

Option 2: Delete via Email Support

If you no longer have access to the app or cannot login, you may request deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to support@vetfala.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Request Account Deletion".
  3. Please send the email from the same email address associated with your VetFala account so we can verify your identity.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account deletion is processed, the following data associated with your user ID will be permanently removed from our database:

  • Personal Information: Your email address, encrypted password, and profile settings.
  • Patient Records: All animal profiles, owner details, and medical history summaries.
  • Clinical Reports: All generated PDF reports, JSON data structure of reports, and any associated text transcripts.
  • Audio Data: Any temporary audio links or references associated with your consultations.

Data Retention Information

While we aim to delete all data immediately upon request, please note the following retention details:

  • Processing Time: In-app deletions are immediate. Email requests are typically processed within 30 days in accordance with GDPR.
  • Backups: Your data may remain in our encrypted secure backups for up to 90 days before being completely overwritten.
  • Legal Obligations: If you have generated invoices or financial documents using the billing feature, we may be required by local tax laws to retain a record of the transaction (but not the clinical details) for a specific period.
