Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

VetFala ("the Company", "We", "Us", or "Our") is committed to protecting Your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

1. Interpretation and Definitions

1.1 Interpretation

The words whose initial letters are capitalized have meanings defined under the following conditions. The following definitions shall have the same meaning regardless of whether they appear in singular or in plural.

1.2 Definitions

  • Account: A unique account created for You to access our Service.
  • Application: Refers to VetFala, the software program provided by the Company.
  • Personal Data: Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Service: Refers to the Application.
  • Service Provider: Third-party companies (such as OpenAI and Supabase) employed by the Company to facilitate the Service.
  • Usage Data: Data collected automatically, such as the duration of a page visit or device identifiers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information, including:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication.
  • Authentication Data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account information.
  • User ID: Automatically generated to identify your account.

2.2 Clinical Data

As a veterinary professional tool, VetFala processes specialized data to provide its core functionality:

  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings of clinical consultations.
  • Transcriptions: Text transcriptions processed from your audio.
  • Clinical Reports: Structured veterinary reports generated by AI.
  • Animal & Owner Information: Pet names, owner names, and medical history provided by You.
  • Procedure & Appointment Data: Medical procedures, billing summaries, and Google Calendar events (if connected).

2.3 Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically. This may include Your Device's IP address, browser type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and diagnostic data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The Company uses Your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service: Including processing audio to generate reports and organizing veterinary records.
  • To manage Your Account: Giving You access to different functionalities available to registered users.
  • For the performance of a contract: To fulfill the services You have requested.
  • To contact You: Via email or push notifications regarding updates or security communications.
  • Service Improvement: To improve AI transcription accuracy and enhance app performance based on usage patterns.

4. Third-Party Services

We share Your personal and clinical information with specific Service Providers to enable app functionality. We do NOT sell Your data.

4.1 OpenAI

Purpose: Audio transcription and AI report generation. OpenAI receives audio recordings and transcribed text to process them into reports.

View OpenAI Privacy Policy

4.2 Supabase

Purpose: Database hosting and authentication. All clinical records and user data are stored on Supabase’s secure cloud infrastructure.

View Supabase Privacy Policy

4.3 Google Services

If You choose to use Google Sign-In or connect Google Calendar, Google will process data according to their Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Security Measures

We use commercially reasonable means to protect Your data, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission (TLS/SSL).
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) ensuring You can only access Your own data.
  • Secure authentication protocols.

Note: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect Your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5.2 Data Location

Your data is processed at the Company's operating offices and on servers (Supabase) located in various jurisdictions. By using the Service, You consent to this transfer.

6. Deleting Your Personal Data

You have the right to delete or request assistance in deleting the Personal Data that We have collected. You may delete Your account at any time through Settings → Account → Delete Account.

This will permanently delete:

  • All clinical reports, transcriptions, and audio files.
  • All animal profiles and medical histories.
  • All billing records and templates.
  • Your user authentication data.

7. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

As VetFala is based in Portugal, users in the European Union have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right to access, rectify, or erase Your personal data.
  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to object to or restrict processing.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time.

8. Professional Use Disclaimer

Important: VetFala is a tool designed for licensed veterinary professionals. AI-generated reports are intended to assist, not replace, professional judgment. Always review and verify all AI-generated content before use in clinical practice.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If We become aware of such data, We take steps to remove it immediately.
