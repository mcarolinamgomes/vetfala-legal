Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2024

VetFala is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email Address: For account creation and authentication
  • User ID: Automatically generated to identify your account
  • Authentication Data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account information

1.2 Clinical Data

As a veterinary professional tool, VetFala processes:

  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings of clinical consultations
  • Transcriptions: Text transcriptions of your audio recordings
  • Clinical Reports: Structured veterinary reports generated from your consultations
  • Animal Information: Pet names, owner names, and medical history
  • Procedure Data: Medical procedures and associated costs
  • Appointment Data: Calendar events and follow-up schedules (if you connect Google Calendar)

1.3 Usage Information

  • Settings & Preferences: Language preferences, template selections
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Process audio recordings to generate clinical reports using AI
  • Store and organize your veterinary records
  • Generate billing summaries and procedure catalogs
  • Sync appointments with your Google Calendar (if connected)
  • Provide multi-language support for reports

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Improve AI transcription and report generation accuracy
  • Enhance user experience and app performance
  • Develop new features based on usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

VetFala uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

3.1 OpenAI

  • Purpose: Audio transcription and AI-powered report generation
  • Data Shared: Audio recordings and transcribed text
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

3.2 Supabase

  • Purpose: Database hosting and user authentication
  • Data Shared: All user data and clinical records
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

3.3 Google Services

  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method
  • Google Calendar: Optional calendar integration for appointments
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Location

Your data is stored securely in Supabase's cloud infrastructure. All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols.

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data
  • Secure authentication using industry-standard protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all your clinical reports and records within the app
  • Edit Your Data: Modify reports, animal profiles, and settings
  • Export Your Data: Download reports as PDFs
  • Delete Your Data: Permanently delete individual records or your entire account

5.2 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently delete:

  • All clinical reports and transcriptions
  • All animal profiles and medical histories
  • All billing records and procedures
  • All templates and appointments
  • Your user account and authentication data

Note: This action cannot be undone.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or clinical data to third parties for marketing purposes.

6.2 Limited Sharing

We only share your data:

  • With third-party service providers (OpenAI, Supabase) necessary for app functionality
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent

7. Children's Privacy

VetFala is designed for use by veterinary professionals and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, for significant changes, we may provide additional notice within the app.

10. Professional Use Disclaimer

Important: VetFala is a professional tool designed for licensed veterinary professionals. The AI-generated reports are intended to assist, not replace, professional veterinary judgment. Always review and verify all AI-generated content before use in clinical practice.

11. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
