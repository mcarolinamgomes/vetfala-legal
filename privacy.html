Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

VetFala (also known as VetScribe) ("we," "us," or "our") operates as a mobile application designed to assist veterinary professionals in generating clinical documentation using Artificial Intelligence. This application is developed and managed by Maria Gomes, a solo developer based in Portugal.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of the clinical data you trust to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using VetFala, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we process several types of data:

A. Personal Information (User Account)

When you register, we collect:

  • Email address.
  • Password (encrypted and managed via our authentication provider).

B. Practice and Billing Information (Optional)

To enable the automatic generation of invoices and billing reports, you may choose to provide specific details about your veterinary practice. This includes:

  • Practice Details: Practice Name, Tax Identification Number (VAT/NIF), and Bank Account Details (IBAN).
  • Client Billing Details: The Tax Identification Number (VAT/NIF) of the animal owners.

Providing this information is optional and is used solely for formatting reports and calculating costs within the application.

C. Clinical and Patient Data

As a tool for veterinary professionals, you input data regarding your patients (animals) and clients (owners). This includes:

  • Audio Recordings: Voice consultations recorded via the app.
  • Transcripts: Text converted from your audio recordings via OpenAI Whisper.
  • Report Data: Structured clinical reports (SOAP, Surgery, etc.), animal names, species, and medical history.
  • Owner Information: Names and contact details of animal owners included in your reports.

Important Note on Consent: You, the Veterinary Professional, act as the Data Controller for your clients' (pet owners) data. It is your responsibility to obtain necessary consent from the pet owner before recording consultations or inputting their data into VetFala.

D. Technical and Usage Data

  • Device information (model, operating system).
  • App usage logs (for security and debugging).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Service Provision: To transcribe audio and generate structured veterinary clinical reports.
  2. Billing Automation: To calculate procedure costs and generate financial summaries using your Practice VAT and IBAN details.
  3. Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account via Supabase Auth.
  4. Data Management: To store and organize patient histories and allow you to search/filter records.
  5. Calendar Integration: To manage appointment scheduling and follow-ups via Google Calendar.

4. AI and Third-Party Data Processing

VetFala utilizes third-party services to provide its core functionality. We have ensured that these partners adhere to strict security standards.

A. OpenAI (Artificial Intelligence)

We use OpenAI (Whisper and GPT-4 models) for:

  • Transcription: Converting voice recordings into text.
  • Report Generation: Formatting text into professional clinical structures.
  • Data Privacy: Your data is sent to OpenAI via our secure server (Edge Functions). OpenAI does not use data submitted via their API to train their models by default.

B. Supabase (Backend Services)

We use Supabase for:

  • Database: Hosting encrypted user and patient data.
  • Authentication: Managing secure user logins.
  • Edge Functions: Processing data securely without exposing API keys to the client.

C. Google Calendar Integration

If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, VetFala will:

  • Access your calendar to find available slots.
  • Create events for follow-up appointments based on your reports.
  • Google User Data Policy: VetFala's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted over secure connections (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Row Level Security (RLS): Our database structure ensures that users can strictly access only their own data.
  • Server-Side Processing: API keys for AI services are never exposed to the mobile client; all processing occurs in secure Edge Functions.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and clinical data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service to you.

  • User Control: You may archive patients or delete specific report sections at any time.
  • Account Deletion: If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below.

7. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users in the European Union have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data (e.g., updating your Practice VAT or IBAN).
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Restriction: Restrict how we process your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@vetfala.com.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information, including Personal Data, is processed in the region where our cloud providers (Supabase) host their servers. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside of your country of residence, provided strict security measures are in place.

9. Children's Privacy

VetFala is intended for use by veterinary professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
